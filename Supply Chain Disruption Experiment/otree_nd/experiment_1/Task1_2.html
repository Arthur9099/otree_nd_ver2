Task 1.2 (b/a): Risk Preference Eliciting, modified from Holt and Laury (2002)

The task below contains 10 different decisions, in each of which you need to make your choices by selecting either Option A or Option B.

After completing the task, the computer will randomly select 1 out of these 10 decisions to determine your potential payoff based on the choice you made in that decision, Option A or Option B. For example, your choice is Option B in decision number 4, and the randomized result is decision number 4; in this case, you have a 40% chance of earning 1750 ECU, added to your extra tasks' payoff; otherwise, you earn nothing.

Each decision has an equal chance of being chosen in the end. You are advised to consider carefully when making your choices so that you have a better chance of earning a higher payoff in this task.
